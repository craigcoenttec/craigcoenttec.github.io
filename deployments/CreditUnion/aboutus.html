About Almost Real Credit Union

Your trusted financial partner for over 50 years

Our Story

Founded in 1973, Almost Real Credit Union began as a small financial cooperative serving local community members. What started as a modest operation with just 200 members has grown into a thriving institution serving over 25,000 members across the region.

As a member-owned cooperative, we operate on a simple principle: people helping people. Unlike traditional banks that focus on profit margins, we exist solely to serve our members' financial needs and help them achieve their goals.

Our Mission & Values

Member-First

Every decision we make is guided by what's best for our members. Your financial success is our primary goal, not generating profits for shareholders.

Community Focused

We reinvest in our local community through sponsorships, donations, and financial education programs that benefit everyone in our region.

Financial Education

We believe informed members make better financial decisions. That's why we offer free workshops, one-on-one counseling, and online resources.

Innovation

While we honor our traditional values, we embrace modern technology to provide convenient, secure banking solutions for today's lifestyle.

Transparency

No hidden fees, no surprises. We believe in clear, honest communication about all our products and services.

Personal Service

You're not just an account number here. Our friendly, knowledgeable staff knows you by name and understands your unique financial needs.
